## Welcome... 🔏

Below you will find a list of the tabletop campaigns I hosted for the FaceplateVTT table along with some resource repositories I put together for free-use.


## Campaign Archives (sorted by latest)

⚠️ Every entry in this list is extremely offensive and unsafe for all audiences.

- God's Teeth by FaceplateVTT (January 4, 2026 - Ongoing)  
https://github.com/Quororque/GodsTeeth-FaceplateVTT-Public  
The most disturbing tabletop campaign ever written, reinterpreted for FaceplateVTT table. What will become of the Teeth?  

- Une nuit à l'opéra by FaceplateVTT (November 15, 2025)  
https://github.com/Quororque/UNAL-FaceplateVTT-Public  
Oneshot based on the 2019 shotgun scenario by mellonbread and William Roy. Includes a complete map of the Haskell Opera House and the tragic story of a TPK due to inexperience and poor decisions.

- Reverberations by FaceplateVTT (September 7 - November 2, 2025)  
https://github.com/Quororque/Reverberations-FaceplateVTT-Public  
A light-hearted one chapter campaign I ran in order to gain experience with FoundryVTT for more serious campaign endavours. Features setting & characters from AMC series Breaking Bad (which I was watching at the time).

## Resources (welcome to use)

- Combat Tokens by FaceplateVTT  
https://github.com/Quororque/CombatTokensByFaceplateVTT  
Combat token overlays ready to be used, inspired by DoorKickers (2014) sprites and created with the help of ChatGPT. I would love to hire a real professional artist to remake this entire thing some time in the future, but for now, this is the most my budget could spare.

- Weapon SFX by FaceplateVTT  
https://github.com/Quororque/WeaponSFXByFaceplateVTT  
Collection of royalty free sound bits all over the internet to be used in any modern combat setting tabletop game imaginable.

- Bystander Tokens by FaceplateVTT   
https://github.com/Quororque/BystanderTokensByFaceplateVTT  
Collection of portraits used in FaceplateVTT games. Most generated by ChatGPT.

- FoundryToPDF by FaceplateVTT  
https://github.com/Quororque/FoundryToPDF  
Python script I use to convert FoundryVTT chat archive exports into PDF/docx format.
